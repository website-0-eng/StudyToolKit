Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to StudyToolKit. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our website and tools, and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

StudyToolKit is designed to be privacy-focused. We do not collect or store:

  • Any text you input into our tools (paraphrasing, summarization, etc.)
  • Any files you upload for conversion
  • Personal information unless voluntarily provided (e.g., through contact forms)

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our services.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use only essential cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie preferences
  • Maintain your session while using our tools

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

4. Data Security

All processing happens in your browser. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to download or save the output.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties. Some tools may use third-party libraries (like jsPDF) that process data locally in your browser.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
